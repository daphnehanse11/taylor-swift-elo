ELO is a rating system originally created for chess. When two items compete, the winner gains points and the loser loses points. The amount depends on their relative rankings:

  • If a lower-ranked album beats a higher-ranked one, it gains more points (upset!)
  • If the favorite wins, fewer points change hands
  • All albums start at 1500 ELO
